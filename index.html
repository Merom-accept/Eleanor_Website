About

Eleanor works at the intersection of scale and precision — her hyper-realistic oil paintings demand both distance and closeness, revealing new detail the longer you look.

Trained in classical technique, she has spent years pushing the boundaries of what oil on canvas can hold. Her works range from intimate large-format canvases to paintings that fill entire rooms — and beyond.

She accepts a small number of commissions each year: canvas paintings, interior murals, exterior facades, and large-scale installations. Every project begins with a conversation.

Medium Oil on Canvas
Scale Up to XL
Surface Canvas · Wall · Facade
